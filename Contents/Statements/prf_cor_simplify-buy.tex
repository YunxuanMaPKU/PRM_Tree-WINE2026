\begin{proof}
By \cref{lem:simplify-sell}, buyer $i$'s optimal action is $x_i = 1$ iff $v_i - p_i + \sum_{j \in \Children(i)} \max_{p_j} G^{(j)}_i(v_i, p_j) \ge 0$. Define:
\[
\ts^\dagger_i(v_i) \;\coloneqq\; v_i + \sum_{j \in \Children(i)} \max_{p_j \ge 0}\; G^{(j)}_i(v_i, p_j).
\]
Then $x_i = 1$ iff $\ts^\dagger_i(v_i) \ge p_i$, which is the threshold strategy in \cref{eq:threshold}. For leaf $i$: $\ts^\dagger_i(v_i) = v_i$.
\qed
\end{proof}
